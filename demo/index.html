About Me

I am native Floridian who started building for the web as a teen in the mid-'90s, so I've been a web developer for about as long as anybody. Co-founding the Tampa Ruby Brigade in 2006 and Suncoast Developers Guild in 2015; I take pride in being a leader in the local tech community. After stints in academia, startups, and as a consulting freelancer, I've found great joy in mentoring and sharing the knowledge gained along the way. I am a father of two, a skeptic, an avid board game player, and entirely too opinionated. When the world gets heavy, you can find me either painting miniatures as catharsis or harnessing my power animal—the panda.
